<p>Description</p> 


<ul>

<li>
<p>Frequency:  The frequency on which the measurement was taken, specified in kHz.</p> 

<li>
<p>Bandwidth:  The measurement bandwidth of the measurement in kHz.</p> 

<li>
<p>Average RSSI: The average power level observed at this frequency in dBm.</p> 

<li>
<p>Peak RSSI: The peak power level observed at this frequency in dBm.</p> 

     </ul>

void LLRP::CFrequencyRSSILevelEntry::assimilateSubParameters ( CErrorDetails *  pError)
virtual

Assimilates the decoded sub-parameters into m_listAllSubParameters.

After fields are decoded, the CDecoder itself takes care of gathering the subparameters into m_listAllSubParameters. Once the end of the enclosing TLV (or message) is reached this assimilateSubParameters() function is called to create parameter refrences from the primary member variables.
